F1 in Schools FAQs

What is F1 in Schools?
F1 in Schools is an international STEM competition where students design, analyze, manufacture, and race miniature CO₂-powered Formula 1 cars.
What age group is F1 in Schools for?
The competition is open to students aged 9–19, typically divided into junior, development, and professional classes.
How are teams judged?
Teams are judged on engineering design, enterprise, verbal presentation, pit display, portfolio, and car performance on track.
How do I get started with a team?
Start by gathering a team of 3–6 members, register on the official F1 in Schools website, and begin developing your car and portfolio materials.
